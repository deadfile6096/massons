What Was Project Blue Book?
Between 1952 and 1969, the United States Air Force conducted a top-secret investigation into Unidentified Flying Objects (UFOs) under the code name Project Blue Book.
12,618 cases were officially recorded.
701 remain unexplained.
Civilian sightings, radar contacts, pilot encounters all logged, analyzed, then buried.
Official goal: To determine if UFOs were a threat to national security.
Unofficial result: To contain the narrative, discredit witnesses, and label truth-seekers as paranoid.

Headquartered at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio
